Notes for Dashboard Diagrams
1.	Country of Survey Takers
  •	Displays the geographic distribution of respondents.
  •	Helps identify which countries contributed most to the survey.
2.	Average Salary by Job Title
  •	Shows the mean annual salary for each job title in the dataset.
  •	Useful for salary benchmarking and understanding compensation trends across roles.
3.	Favourite Programming Languages
  •	Illustrates the most preferred programming languages among survey participants.
  •	Can guide learning priorities or tech stack insights.
4.	Difficulty to Break Into Data
  •	Indicates how respondents perceive the challenge of entering the data profession.
  •	Highlights potential barriers for new data professionals.
5.	Happiness with Work/Life Balance
  •	Visualizes respondents’ satisfaction with their work-life balance.
  •	Useful for assessing company culture and employee wellbeing trends.
6.	Happiness with Salary
  •	Shows the level of satisfaction with current compensation.
  •	Helps identify potential gaps between expected and actual salaries.
